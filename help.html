Travel tips

  • for convenience make advance reservation of hotels, transport and local sightseeing of the city and journey on Luxury Trains.

  • Carrying authorized map of the State and City to which you are traveling to.

  • Carry woolen cloths in winter and walking shoes for all season.

  • Carrying all essential medicines as advice by your Doctor.

  • Travel insurance can protect you of any un-seen hazards.

  • Avoid taking services of unknown and unauthorized guides/touts.

  • Avoid heavy baggage and prefer use light weight bags with wheels to avoid any inconvenience.

  • Before visiting take help line Number. of Tourism Department, Police and Medical Services.

  • Inform your allergies and food habits in the hotel or restaurant before you order for meals or beverages.

  • Avoid carrying expensive items like jewelry, gold and valuables of high worth.

  • Make purchase from authorized shops and take proper vouchers of purchases made with telephone number and identification of salesman or owner of the shop.

  • Carrying safe drinking water and dry eatables while travelling.

  • Make a list of your belongings which you are carrying with you with identification marks or names of brands, etc.

  • In case of any inconvenience during the stay at RTDC hotels, Please contact RTDC Customer Grievance Cell on e-mail info@rtdc.in

  • In case of ill-health during the stay in RTDC hotels or travelling on luxury trains, Please contact Manager immediately for help.

  • Always carry a photocopy of your travel documents i.e. Passport, Visa or Travel Pass and inform the police immediately on the loss any of such documents.

  • Give proper wake-up call at hotel reception as and when you have to catch early morning or late night connecting Bus or Train.

  • Avoid getting extra familiar with hotel staff or any other guests during your stay and in case of any abnormal or indecent behavior, report to Hotel Manager or RTDC Customer

  • Carry Indian Currency or credit card (useable in india) to clear your miscellaneous service Bills.

  • Avoid booking of ticket or hotel booking through unauthorized agents
